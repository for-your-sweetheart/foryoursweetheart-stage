]]> The National Heart Week & World Heart Day is Singapore Heart Foundation’s annual event that aims to increase public awareness and promote preventive measures to reduce cardiovascular disease and stroke. This signature event uses a fun and light-hearted approach to encourage individuals to take care of their precious hearts. There will be activities related to cardiovascular disease prevention such as health screenings, a healthy cooking demonstration, heart-health products and service exhibitions, workouts and many more!

]]> World Diabetes Day, created by the International Diabetes Federation and the World Health Organization is commemorated annually on 14 November. In Singapore, Diabetes Singapore has been the main organiser of World Diabetes Day events since 1991. The annual World Diabetes Day will be held on 4 November 2018, with this year’s theme being “The Family & Diabetes – Together We Can Beat Diabetes”, because we aim to highlight the importance of family and support network in the fight against diabetes.
