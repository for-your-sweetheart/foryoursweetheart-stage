ITE Fiesta 2019

Date: 28 July 2019

Time: 9am-5pm

Venue: ITE College West

Join us at ITE College West  at 9.30am  on July 28, 2019 in Blk 1, Lvl 5 Rm 1520 to learn about Managing Diabetes with Dr. Yew Tong Wei (Consultant and Diabetes Clinical Service, Director for the Division of Endocrinology at NUH).

Visit our For Your SweetHeart booth at the Piazza and stand a chance to take home a limited edition For Your SweetHeart bracelet!

The Fiesta also features an exciting array of activities  such as innovative projects, exhilarating carnival stalls, free workshops, fun fringe activities and breath-taking student performances!

All are welcome and admission is FREE!
