www.diabetes.org.sg

Diabetes Singapore is a non-profit charitable organisation affiliated to the International Diabetes Federation and the National Council of Social Service. Its mission is to raise awareness and to provide education, counselling and support to people with diabetes, their families and the community in order to empower them to lead a healthy and productive life.

www.myheart.org.sg

The Singapore Heart Foundation is a non-profit charitable organisation at the forefront of the battle against heart disease in Singapore. Its mission is to promote heart health, prevent and reduce disability and death due to cardiovascular disease and stroke.
